Reduce the amount of misplaced waste in the oceans

Turning the tide


With over 5 trillion pieces of trash, it's never been more important to clean our oceans. With hundreds of millions of animals dying each year, we strive to eliminate these hazardous materials. Our website ClearCurrent powered by an AI model strives to reduce and recycle the trash inside of the ocean. Our model has a 95 percent accuracy compared to the human error which is inconsistent. By using our AI model, we are providing efficient ways to prevent Global Warming and save our earth from pollution.
