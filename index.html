My Mission

To reduce human trash, especially recyclable plastics, in oceanic ecosystems

CleanCurrent


It is common knowledge that trash in our oceans is a big problem. The damage to wildlife and coastal communities is dangerously high. Although completely removing the trash in our oceans is a complicated problem that cannot be solved at once, CleanCurrent eliminates a significant portion. With its highly accurate AI model, CleanCurrent will ensure that no more recyclable waste will make it into the ocean.

Ocean pollution

Plastic pollution is a growing problem in the world's oceans, with a significant amount of plastic waste ending up in landfills and harming marine life. Microplastics in seafood can also affect humans, and coastal communities that rely on the ocean are also impacted. Effective waste management and recycling practices are needed to address the issue and reduce the impact of plastic pollution on the environment and human life.

Current solution

The world has recently been recognizing the environmental and health risks associated with the excessive use of plastics. Many countries have implemented various measures such as plastic bag bans, plastic taxes, and improved waste management systems to reduce plastic pollution. Additionally, there has been a growing awareness, participation, and initiative to develop eco-friendly alternatives to plastics, such as biodegradable options.

CleanCurrent's contribution

CleanCurrent's AI model is capable of processing a vast number of images in real-time, making it a powerful tool for automated waste sorting and recycling operations. By accurately identifying recyclable materials, CleanCurrent ensures that no valuable recyclable waste ends up in the ocean. The advanced AI capabilities of CleanCurrent allows it to drive positive change by promoting sustainable waste management practices and fostering a circular economy.
